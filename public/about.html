- ABOUT THE PRODUCT -

The Selfstart recommendation engine is a tool to help people find the perfect educational resources, specific to their needs and preferences. The problem we want to solve is having to spend hours looking for the best how-to guide, instructional video, or step-by-step article that fully met our preferences. After frustration or time restraints, we're left conceding to something that doesn't completely fulfill our needs. The vision of the Selfstart recommendation engine is to be the go-to spot for any search related to self education.
